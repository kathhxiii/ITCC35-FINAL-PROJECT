Generika Pharmacy

Pabayo-Chaves St., Barangay 5, Cagayan de Oro, 9000 Misamis Oriental

Telephone: 0932 886 3019

Store Hours: open all day

Generika Drugstore was founded with the vision of empowering Filipinos by providing them access to quality, affordable generic medicines. When Generika opened its first drugstore at Montillano, Muntilupa City in 2004, it became the pioneer champion for quality generic medicines in the pharma retail space. Since then it has made a positive impact on the lives of many Filipinos, and has received numerous awards, recognizing its positive contribution to the promotion of the health and well-being of Filipinos.
